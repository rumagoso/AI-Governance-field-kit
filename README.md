# AI Governance Field Kit

**Practitioner-grade tools for governing AI systems in the real world.**

A growing collection of open frameworks, probes, templates, and reference artefacts for AI governance work — built from inside ISMS, audit, and compliance practice, not from outside it.

---

## What lives here

This repository will become the umbrella home for a portfolio of frameworks and tools currently published across separate repositories. Consolidation is in progress.

**Frameworks**

- **WBSC — Worldview & Belief System Card.** An open standard for AI transparency. Published via CSA Portugal, October 2025. → [github.com/rumagoso/worldview-belief-system-card](https://github.com/rumagoso/worldview-belief-system-card)
- **WBSC-PL — WBSC Probe Library.** A structured library of prompts for behaviourally sensing AI systems. CC0. → [github.com/rumagoso/wbsc-probe-library](https://github.com/rumagoso/wbsc-probe-library)
- **TRACE — Trusted Registry for Autonomous Connected Entities.** A position paper on agent accountability and chain-of-custody for AI actions. NIST NCCoE comment submitted April 2026.

**Templates and reference artefacts**

- AI Supplier Assessment Template — ten-question template for evaluating AI vendors. CC BY 4.0.
- RACI Matrix for Agentic AI Governance — Portuguese, CC BY 4.0.
- Further templates and reference materials to follow.

---

## Who this is for

- **ISMS managers, DPOs, auditors, and compliance officers** who need practical artefacts for ISO 27001, NIS2, GDPR, DORA, ISO 22301, and ISO 42001 work that touches AI systems.
- **AI governance practitioners** building the floor, not the ceiling.
- **Researchers and educators** looking for empirically tested probes, frameworks, and case material.

The tools here are designed to be auditable, citable, and usable — not to advance a particular theoretical position.
